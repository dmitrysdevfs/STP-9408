About the Game

Donut Boom! is a relaxing yet mentally engaging mobile puzzle game where your goal is to sort donuts by color and stack them into the correct boxes. The concept is simple but surprisingly addictive: organize the colorful treats with as few moves as possible to clear each level. Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages, Donut Boom combines intuitive gameplay with charming visuals and satisfying sound design.
