Levels & Difficulty

Donut Boom! is designed with a gentle learning curve that welcomes beginners while still challenging seasoned puzzle lovers. The game currently features over 50 levels (with more added regularly), starting with simple layouts and gradually introducing more complex mechanics. Early levels are designed to teach you the basics—like sorting donuts into empty boxes and matching by color. As you advance, the puzzles increase in complexity with tighter space, more donut colors, and fewer available moves. Mid-tier levels introduce layered stacks, decoy donuts, and locked positions that require careful sequencing and forward planning. Advanced levels are truly a test of strategy, logic, and memory, where one wrong move could force a restart. But don't worry—every level is designed to be fair and solvable without needing hints or purchases. If you’re a perfectionist, you’ll love replaying levels to earn 3-star scores by using the fewest moves possible. The difficulty system keeps things fresh without overwhelming players. It’s a sweet progression journey—from casual sorting to masterful stacking. Plus, seasonal bonus levels and special challenge modes are coming soon to keep the game exciting and full of surprises!
