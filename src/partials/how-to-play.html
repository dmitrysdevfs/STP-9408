How to Play

  1. Tap a box to select the top donut.
  2. Tap another box to place it—only if the donut color matches or the box is empty.
  3. Match all donuts in one box by color to complete the level.
  4. Earn stars based on your moves—fewer moves, more stars!
  5. Advance to tougher puzzles with limited box space and more colors.
