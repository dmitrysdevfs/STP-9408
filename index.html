About the Game

Donut Boom! is a relaxing yet mentally engaging mobile puzzle game where your goal is to sort donuts by color and stack them into the correct boxes. The concept is simple but surprisingly addictive: organize the colorful treats with as few moves as possible to clear each level. Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages, Donut Boom combines intuitive gameplay with charming visuals and satisfying sound design. Whether you're looking for a stress-free way to unwind or a logic challenge to sharpen your brain, this game has something for everyone. With smooth drag-and-drop controls, no time limits, and a growing collection of levels, Donut Boom offers an endlessly enjoyable experience—where every tap feels rewarding. As you progress, new obstacles like limited box space and tricky color patterns keep things interesting. It's the perfect mix of calm and cleverness. Get ready to enter a world where logic meets sweetness!
