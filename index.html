About the Game

Donut Boom! is a relaxing yet mentally engaging mobile puzzle game where your goal is to sort donuts by color and stack them into the correct boxes. The concept is simple but surprisingly addictive: organize the colorful treats with as few moves as possible to clear each level. Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages, Donut Boom combines intuitive gameplay with charming visuals and satisfying sound design. Whether you're looking for a stress-free way to unwind or a logic challenge to sharpen your brain, this game has something for everyone. With smooth drag-and-drop controls, no time limits, and a growing collection of levels, Donut Boom offers an endlessly enjoyable experience—where every tap feels rewarding. As you progress, new obstacles like limited box space and tricky color patterns keep things interesting. It's the perfect mix of calm and cleverness. Get ready to enter a world where logic meets sweetness!

How to Play

  1. Tap a box to select the top donut.
  2. Tap another box to place it—only if the donut color matches or the box is empty.
  3. Match all donuts in one box by color to complete the level.
  4. Earn stars based on your moves—fewer moves, more stars!
  5. Advance to tougher puzzles with limited box space and more colors.

Levels & Difficulty

Donut Boom! is designed with a gentle learning curve that welcomes beginners while still challenging seasoned puzzle lovers. The game currently features over 50 levels (with more added regularly), starting with simple layouts and gradually introducing more complex mechanics. Early levels are designed to teach you the basics—like sorting donuts into empty boxes and matching by color. As you advance, the puzzles increase in complexity with tighter space, more donut colors, and fewer available moves. Mid-tier levels introduce layered stacks, decoy donuts, and locked positions that require careful sequencing and forward planning. Advanced levels are truly a test of strategy, logic, and memory, where one wrong move could force a restart. But don't worry—every level is designed to be fair and solvable without needing hints or purchases. If you’re a perfectionist, you’ll love replaying levels to earn 3-star scores by using the fewest moves possible. The difficulty system keeps things fresh without overwhelming players. It’s a sweet progression journey—from casual sorting to masterful stacking. Plus, seasonal bonus levels and special challenge modes are coming soon to keep the game exciting and full of surprises!

FAQ

  • Is Donut Boom! free to play?

    Yes, Donut Boom! is completely free to download and play. The core gameplay, including all available levels, can be accessed without any cost. We do include optional ads and in-app purchases to support the game’s development and future updates. If you choose to watch ads or purchase hints or power-ups, it can enhance your experience but is never required. You can enjoy a full and satisfying puzzle experience even if you never spend a cent. We believe games should be fun, fair, and accessible to everyone.

  • How do I play Donut Boom?

    The goal is to sort colorful donuts into boxes so that each box contains donuts of the same color. You tap one box to pick up the top donut, then tap another box to drop it—only if the top color matches or the box is empty. You win the level when all donuts are properly grouped. Early levels help you learn the rules, while later levels test your strategy. There are no timers or penalties, so you can take your time to plan your moves. It’s relaxing, logical, and highly satisfying.

  • Do I need an internet connection to play?

    No, you don’t need a constant internet connection to enjoy Donut Boom. Once downloaded, the game can be played entirely offline, making it perfect for travel, commuting, or relaxing anywhere without Wi-Fi. However, features like ad rewards or cloud save do require an internet connection. We designed the core experience to be available at all times, regardless of network status. Whether you're online or offline, the puzzle fun never stops.

  • What devices are compatible with Donut Boom?

    Donut Boom is currently available for Android devices running version 6.0 or later. It’s optimized to run smoothly on both phones and tablets, adapting beautifully to different screen sizes. We are actively exploring a version for iOS devices, which may be released in future updates. If your Android device meets the minimum requirements, you’ll get full functionality and a seamless experience. For best performance, we recommend keeping your device updated to the latest Android version.

  • Are there any time limits or pressure while playing?

    Nope! Donut Boom is designed to be a relaxing and pressure-free puzzle experience. There are no countdowns, time limits, or lives that run out—you can take as much time as you need on each level. This allows players to focus on strategy and logic without feeling rushed. It’s a great game to unwind with after a busy day. Our goal is to provide a fun, stress-free environment that makes every tap feel rewarding.

  • How difficult do the levels get?

    The game starts off easy to help you learn the mechanics, but the challenge ramps up gradually. As you progress, you’ll encounter more boxes, more colors, and tighter spaces, which require careful thinking. Higher levels may introduce limited moves, tricky stacking sequences, and obstacles like locked donuts. These advanced puzzles are designed to challenge your logic without feeling unfair. If you enjoy thinking ahead and solving clever problems, you’ll love the later stages of Donut Boom.

If you didn’t get three stars the first time, you can always go back and try to optimize your strategy. This makes the game perfect for completionists and perfectionists.
